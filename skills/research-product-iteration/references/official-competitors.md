# Official Competitors

Prefer official product pages and official help/blog pages.

## Default set

- Semantic Scholar
  Look for feeds, dashboard, citations, recommendations, and alerts.
- ResearchRabbit
  Look for collections, graph exploration, and ongoing discovery workflows.
- Litmaps
  Look for citation maps, tracking, and update monitoring.
- Elicit
  Look for structured comparison, reports, and evidence organization.
- SciSpace
  Look for reading assistance, per-paper guidance, and PDF interaction.

## Signal Checklist

Capture only what matters for product iteration:

- How they help users find the next relevant paper
- How they help users decide whether a paper is worth reading
- How they support ongoing tracking instead of one-off search
- How they structure summaries, comparisons, or reading assistance
- Which part can be adapted here with low implementation risk

## Reporting Style

- 3 to 5 signals total
- each signal tied to a named product
- no generic market language
- end with one bounded improvement candidate
